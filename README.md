# Portuguese UCL Winners

I've always felt that the UEFA Champions League is the most prestigious award in football — even more so than the World Cup. Having Portuguese players winning it fills me with immense pride.

Because of that, I created this website to share with all of you the records of every Portuguese football player who has had the honor of lifting this trophy, from Benfica's back-to-back triumphs in the early 1960s all the way to the present day.

## Live Website

[ucl-pt-winners.vercel.app](https://ucl-pt-winners.vercel.app)

## Open Data
